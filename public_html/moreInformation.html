There's a student dead at the library, and the whole campus is asking one question:"Who Dunn It?" In this thrilling account of life on the campus of a small college in the middle of Iowa, interviewers search for the answer to that very question. Along the way they discover secrets of students and teachers and somehow find some humor in this grim situation.
